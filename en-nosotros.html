This is us


Since 1993 we have been offering in Costa Rica the best brands with the latest trends worldwide for new lighting technologies that help improve the environment by saving energy and quality of life for users.


Arte en Luz is a pioneer in bringing the best in innovation and technological development to Engineers, Architects, Consultants, Designers, Developers and Construction and Electromechanical Companies, so your lighting projects are a success.



Servicio Integral

Arte en Luz representa y distribuye marcas reconocidas y es importador directo de las fábricas.
